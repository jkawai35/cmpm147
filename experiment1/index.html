Experiment 1 - Song Title Generation

Description

Generates song titles and possible concepts that can lead to ideas. Idea is that these ideas are generated by robots who don't have a clear sense of emotion hence some of the concept explainations may not fit well with the title.

Technical

Draws randoms elements from a collection of arrays and inserts them at various points in a template string that represents both the song title and the concept explaination.

Reflection

It was a reasonably smooth process changing the filler ideas and getting the messages to be created properly.
